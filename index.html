Checkers

- Checker pieces can only move once diagonally
- It may only move one way to the other side of the board unless it is crowned
- Crowned pieces may move diagonally in any direction
- It may eat the opponent's piece it there is an empty space on the other side
- Click the piece you want to move, and then click the open space that you would like to move to
